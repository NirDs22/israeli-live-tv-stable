# Legal Boundaries

Israeli Live TV Stable is designed for legal live TV playback only.

## Not Allowed

- Pirated IPTV links.
- Paid TV channels without authorization.
- DRM bypass.
- Authentication bypass.
- Fake device IDs, tokens, cookies, sessions, or headers.
- Cloudflare, anti-bot, geo-blocking, or access-control bypass.
- Scraping protected/private APIs.
- Reverse engineering private playback APIs.

## User Sources

Users are responsible for any source they add to `user_sources.json`.

Only add URLs you are legally allowed to access and play in Kodi.

## TVHeadend

TVHeadend support is for user-owned legal reception or user-owned legal local sources, such as a properly configured local tuner setup.

## Bundled Sources

Bundled direct stream URLs may be added only when they are legal, public, stable enough, and backed by official evidence. If uncertain, the addon must not bundle the source.
